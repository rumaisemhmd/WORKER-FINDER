Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on “Sign Up” and choose whether you're a user or a worker. Follow the steps to complete your profile.

How do bookings work?

Users can send a booking or site visit request. Once accepted by the worker, payment is required to confirm.

How is payment handled?

Users pay upfront. Funds are held until the work is completed and OTP verified. Then it is credited to the worker’s wallet.

How can I withdraw money?

Workers can request a withdrawal in the Wallet section. Bank details are required, and the minimum amount is ₹500.

Who do I contact if I face an issue?

See the contact section below. We respond within 24 hours.
