1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data when you register or use Worker Finder:

  • Personal details (full name, phone number, email address)
  • Professional details (skills, experience, wage, service hours)
  • Uploaded images, ratings, and reviews
  • Booking and transaction history
  • Bank details for withdrawals (account number, IFSC, etc.)
  • OTP verification data
  • Device and browser information (used for session security)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your profile to users looking for skilled workers
  • To manage and confirm bookings, payments, and work completions
  • To process OTP verification and prevent fraud
  • To handle wallet balance and withdrawal requests
  • To send notifications related to bookings, payments, and profile activity
  • To improve the platform based on feedback and usage analytics

3. Payment & Wallet Information

All payment data is securely processed. We do not store card or UPI information. Wallet balances and earnings are visible only to the respective worker and are managed internally. Withdrawal requests require valid bank details and password verification for safety.

4. Who Can See Your Data

Only verified users with active bookings can view your contact information. Public profile information (e.g. name, skills, rating) is visible to logged-in users.

5. Location and Session Data

We may use location-based filtering to show nearby workers. We also store session data to enhance login security and prevent unauthorized access.

6. Data Security

We follow strict data protection practices and encryption methods. Only authorized staff can access sensitive data. OTPs and passwords are securely stored using hashing and encryption techniques.

7. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your profile at any time. If you wish to permanently delete your account and associated data, contact our support team.

8. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be notified via email or dashboard. Continued use of the platform indicates your acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact Us
